  • Frog Design Experience Tiles Set

    Cuben Fiber Bags and Wallets

    Extremely lightweight and durable, Cuben fiber is a challenging and rewarding fabric to work with. It is measurably stronger for its weight than nearly any other commercially available product.

    Featured projects were designed and sold under the High Above label. The pieces shown here synthesize specialty, highly technical material with urban functionality.

    Cuben is waterproof and significantly lighter than the heavy truck-tarp interior linings which are commonly used in waterproof bags.

    Materials:

    • Woven nylon faced Cuben Fiber
    • 2 lb 1/4" EVA foam
    • ITW Nexus Onyx buckles
